为什么要进行OCP保护，存在哪些问题

”’ For traditional PWM converter, during over current condition, duty cycle is reduced to limit the current. For LLC resonant converter, it is working with variable frequency control, the over current protection is different. In LLC resonant converter, the impedance of the resonant tank is pretty small during normal operation. This means the current could reach very high level in very short time. This characteristic makes over current protection design for LLC resonant converter very critical. “’

Yang, B & Lee, F.C.Y. & Concannon, M. (2003). Over current protection methods for LLC resonant converter. 605 - 609 vol.2. 10.1109/APEC.2003.1179276. OCP保护的主要作用是，当机器异常时，保证MOSFET的安全，确保机器状态可控。 安全工作区

关断过程的Miller Plateau

Miller Plateau是由GD间的结电容造成的，而结电容Cgd非线性，从下面两张图可以看出，在Vds从零开始爬上到Vbus过程中。Cgd的非线性在Vds电压低时较大，对驱动损耗而言，开通时刻Vds应保持为0V，而关断是硬关断，这个过程是无法避免的。

  1. 起初的100ns仅爬升6V，而这短时间对应Vgs Miller Plateau的时间；

  2. 之后的100ns从6V爬升到了410V。

高频时的类Droop特性是否有作用

主要思想是，在高频时，由于输入电流较大，在输入Vcc电源上串入小电阻，电流大时电阻上压降增加，以此来等效降驱动电压，进而减小驱动损耗。
